Need Things To-Do?

Museum of Modern Art

It's easy to spend hours studying the 33,000+ works at SFMOMA, which houses one of the world's greatest collections of modern and contemporary art and is undoubtedly one of the top museums to visit in San Francisco.

See More!

Aquarium of the Bay!

The Aquarium of the Bay welcomes visitors to meet approximately 20,000 marine animals of all shapes and sizes. Discover the diversity of Northern California's marine life, including sharks, rays, jellyfish, and octopi – and maybe even an otter or two!

See More!

Golden Gate Park

The Golden Gate Park, situated in the center of San Francisco, is a grassy oasis amidst the hustle and bustle of the city, with over 1,000 acres of pristine lawns, clear lakes, and all kinds of rare, gorgeous plants.

See More!

Sail the Bay

San Francisco is a premier sailing destination with strong, steady winds all year. From the Golden Gate Bridge and Alcatraz Island to the bustling waterfront and metropolitan skyline, the bay offers a unique viewpoint on the city's many icons.

See More!

Alcatraz

Built on a ledge in the frigid waters of San Francisco Bay, Alcatraz was only for the toughest convicts – and legends of a handful who managed to elude the inexorable have captivated pop culture for decades.
